PostChat

PostChat is a tool that analyzes chat messages from Twitch and YouTube livestreams or VODs. Paste a video URL, and PostChat will extract the conversation, identify key personas, and generate summaries of audience behavior and engagement.

Whether you're a streamer, researcher, or just curious about community dynamics, PostChat helps you understand what your audience is really saying.
